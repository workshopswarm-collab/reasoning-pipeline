# Retrospective

Use this template for resolved-case review in any current `vault/50-retrospectives/` subfolder.

## What was being evaluated

State the original case, question, recommendation, or research thread.

## What recommendation, lean, or process was used

Describe what the pipeline actually did or concluded.

## What happened in reality

State the observed outcome or what became clear after resolution.

## Which inputs were high signal

List the sources, findings, assumptions, or drivers that helped.

## Which inputs were misleading

List the sources, findings, assumptions, or drivers that pushed the pipeline the wrong way.

## What was missing

State the evidence, framing, or process step that would have improved the outcome.

## False-signal review

List the signals that looked important but should have been downweighted or ignored.

## Missed-signal review

List the signals that should have been weighted earlier or more heavily.

## Source and input-quality review

Explain whether source quality, evidence selection, or provenance handling helped or hurt.

## Conflict review

If there was disagreement in the pipeline, record:
- what type of conflict it was
- which side had better evidence or logic
- whether the disagreement revealed a reusable lesson

## Agent / workflow review

Note whether any role, handoff, or stage in the pipeline performed especially well or poorly.

## Methodology adjustments

State what should change in future research, weighting, retrieval, or escalation behavior.

## Candidate durable lessons for stable layers

List lessons that may deserve promotion into `30-drivers/`, `10-domains/`, or canonical entity notes after review.
