# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Alliance research should focus on commitment credibility, burden sharing, deterrence signaling, and whether member coordination is deepening or weakening.

## Core conclusions

- Alliances matter through both formal commitments and perceived credibility.
- Burden sharing and political cohesion are recurring fault lines.
