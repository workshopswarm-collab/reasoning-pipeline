# Summary

Alliances are one of the main geopolitical coordination mechanisms because they aggregate deterrence, burden sharing, and signaling.

## Key facts extracted

- NATO is a military alliance with collective-defense structure.
- Alliance credibility depends on both formal commitments and member willingness.
- Alliances are strategic signaling systems as much as military arrangements.
