# Summary

Sanctions are a major geopolitical mechanism for coercion short of force, affecting trade, finance, diplomacy, and strategic behavior.

## Key facts extracted

- Sanctions can be economic, diplomatic, financial, and other forms of restriction.
- UN and unilateral sanctions differ in basis and political meaning.
- Sanctions are intended to impose costs to change target behavior.

## Why this source may matter

Sanctions are recurring drivers in country, conflict, energy, and trade-related markets.
