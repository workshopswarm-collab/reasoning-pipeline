# Summary

Robotics matters because it turns AI and control systems into real-world physical action, where operational reliability and cost become decisive.

## Key facts extracted

- Robotics combines sensing, control, and mechanical actuation.
- Real-world deployment is constrained by reliability, safety, and environment complexity.
- Robotics is an applied AI domain with slower but more tangible commercialization paths.
