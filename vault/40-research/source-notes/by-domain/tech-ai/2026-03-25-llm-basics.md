# Summary

Large language models are now the core technical object for many AI markets because they connect model capability, deployment, alignment, benchmarking, and productization.

## Key facts extracted

- Modern LLMs are typically transformer-based models trained on large corpora.
- They generalize across tasks like generation, summarization, coding, and reasoning.
- Fine-tuning, RLHF, and benchmark competition shape deployment quality.

## Why this source may matter

LLM markets are not only about raw capability; they are also about training scale, alignment, benchmarking, and product integration.
