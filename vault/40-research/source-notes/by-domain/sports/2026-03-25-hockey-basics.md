# Summary

Hockey is a high-variance, relatively low-scoring team sport where goaltending, star skaters, depth, and playoff structure all materially affect outcomes.

## Key facts extracted

- Scoring is lower than in many sports, increasing variance effects.
- Team structure, special teams, and goaltending matter heavily.
- League/tournament context changes evaluation.
