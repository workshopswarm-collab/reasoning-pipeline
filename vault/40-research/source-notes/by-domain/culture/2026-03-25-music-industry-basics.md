# Summary

The music industry is an attention-and-distribution system shaped by streaming, touring, ownership, fan intensity, and release cadence.

## Key facts extracted

- Recorded music, publishing, touring, and branding interact.
- Streaming and platform distribution changed revenue and attention structure.
- Artist-level fandom can be a major economic and narrative driver.
