# Summary

Crypto exchanges are core market-structure institutions because they control fiat access, custody assumptions, trading liquidity, and increasingly regulatory exposure.

## Key facts extracted

- Exchanges connect crypto assets to fiat and other digital assets.
- Market structure differs between custodial, brokerage-style, and decentralized exchange models.
- Regulation, withdrawals, custody, and liquidity structure matter materially.
