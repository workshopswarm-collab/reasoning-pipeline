# Summary

GLI frames 2025 as a major transition year for crypto, emphasizing movement from regulatory uncertainty toward clearer legal frameworks and broader institutional adoption.

## Key facts extracted

- U.S. regulation shifted materially in 2025 toward a more crypto-permissive posture.
- Stablecoin legislation and clearer SEC/CFTC boundary-setting are described as major market-structuring events.
- Institutional participation expanded alongside regulatory clarity.
- The EU's MiCA regime is presented as a major global framework, with other jurisdictions diverging in approach.
- DeFi growth continues, but security and operational risk remain core constraints.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- The source describes 2025 as a "watershed moment" for digital assets.
- It highlights stablecoin regulation, SEC/CFTC jurisdiction, and ETF/custody developments as major catalysts.
- It notes growing public-company bitcoin treasury adoption and expanded ETF assets.
