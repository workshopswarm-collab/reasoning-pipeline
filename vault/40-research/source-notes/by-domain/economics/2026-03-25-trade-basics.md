# Summary

International trade is a core macro system linking growth, industrial policy, geopolitics, and supply-chain dependence.

## Key facts extracted

- Trade enables specialization and cross-border exchange.
- It is shaped by tariffs, comparative advantage, logistics, and policy.
- Trade dynamics often interact directly with geopolitical rivalry.
