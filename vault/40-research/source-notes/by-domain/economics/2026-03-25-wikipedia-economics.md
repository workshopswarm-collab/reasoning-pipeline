# Summary

Economics studies production, distribution, and consumption, with major prediction-market relevance concentrated in macro indicators, policy transmission, and agent behavior under changing incentives.

## Key facts extracted

- Economics spans both micro behavior and macro system dynamics.
- Key recurring variables include inflation, growth, labor, savings, investment, and public policy.
- Macroeconomic systems are tightly shaped by institutions and policy.

## Evidence directly stated by source
