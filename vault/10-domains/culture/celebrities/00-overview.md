# Synthesis summary

Celebrity research should focus on public attention, narrative volatility, brand economics, and platform/media amplification.

## Core conclusions

- Celebrity is a market-moving social asset.
- Attention is not identical to positive sentiment.
- Media cycles and platform amplification can change outcomes faster than fundamentals.
