# Synthesis summary

Film research should focus on release timing, wide vs limited distribution, franchise/narrative strength, and how commercial schedule changes interact with audience attention.

## Core conclusions

- Timing is a first-class variable in film outcomes.
- Release-strategy changes can be predictive signals.
- Film markets combine narrative attention with calendar and distribution mechanics.

## Main evidence clusters

- release calendar
- distribution breadth
- franchise and star attention
- schedule changes
- audience/commercial conversion
