# Synthesis summary

Sanctions research should focus on legal basis, enforcement scope, financial and trade channels, and whether imposed costs are likely to alter state or organizational behavior.

## Core conclusions

- Sanctions are among the most reusable geopolitical drivers.
- They matter through enforcement, circumvention, and spillover channels.
- Sanctions often interact with energy, banking, and trade-system fragility.

## Main evidence clusters

- legal basis and scope
- trade and finance restrictions
- enforcement quality
- target adaptation and circumvention
- spillover effects
