# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Energy-geopolitics research should focus on producer power, chokepoints, supply resilience, sanctions exposure, and how energy dependence shapes state behavior.

## Core conclusions

- Energy is a strategic system, not just a commodity market.
- Producer concentration and chokepoints remain critical drivers.
