# Overview summary

Products is the subdomain where model capability turns into user-facing or enterprise-facing offerings through packaging, pricing, workflow integration, distribution, and platform design. It matters because many outcomes in AI are determined less by raw model quality than by how capabilities are translated into usable products.

## Why this area matters

Products matter because product structure determines adoption, retention, monetization, and switching behavior. Researchers need a dedicated products subdomain so they can separate model-level progress from packaging, release strategy, tiering, platform integration, and workflow fit.

## Common conflicts or failure modes

- treating launch attention as durable adoption
- ignoring pricing, packaging, and integration advantages
- conflating model brand, product family, and exact release version
