# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

AI regulation research should focus on jurisdictional divergence, safety requirements, model/deployment thresholds, and whether rules constrain or legitimize adoption.

## Core conclusions

- Regulation is now part of the AI product and market environment, not an external side issue.
- Divergent regulatory regimes can create asymmetric winners and constraints.
