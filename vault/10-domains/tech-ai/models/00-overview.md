# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

AI model research should separate raw capability, benchmark performance, alignment/safety behavior, deployment economics, and product integration.

## Core conclusions

- Capability alone is not the full market story.
- Benchmarks, alignment, and deployment quality can diverge.
- Model research needs to track both technical progress and product usefulness.

## Main evidence clusters

- training and architecture
- benchmarks and evals
- alignment and safety
- deployment economics
- product integration
