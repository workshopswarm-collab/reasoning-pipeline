# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Election research should focus on institutions, timing rules, candidate/party competition, polling, turnout assumptions, and legal constraints.

## Core conclusions

- Elections are process-constrained, not free-form.
- Polling, turnout, and institutional rules all matter materially.
