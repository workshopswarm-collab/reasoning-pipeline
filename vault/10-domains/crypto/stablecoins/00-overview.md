# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Stablecoins should be treated as core crypto market infrastructure. Research should focus on reserves, redemptions, regulation, payment usage, trading settlement, and issuer concentration.

## Core conclusions

- Stablecoins are a structural liquidity layer for crypto markets.
- Reserve design and redemption confidence are central, not secondary.
- Regulation matters because stablecoins sit close to traditional money-like functions.
- Concentration in a few issuers makes category-level shocks potentially market-wide.

## Main evidence clusters

- reserve backing and redemption structure
- issuer concentration
- payment and settlement usage
- regulation and legal treatment
- integration with exchanges and DeFi

## Missing information

- issuer-specific reserve notes
- stablecoin market-share tracking
- chain distribution and DeFi dependency
