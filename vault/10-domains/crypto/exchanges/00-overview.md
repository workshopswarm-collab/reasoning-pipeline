# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Exchange research should focus on liquidity, custody, fiat access, withdrawal structure, regulatory exposure, and exchange-vs-chain dependency.

## Core conclusions

- Exchanges are a major chokepoint in crypto market structure.
- Custody and withdrawal rights matter as much as trading access.
- Regulatory action against exchanges can propagate across asset prices and market participation.
