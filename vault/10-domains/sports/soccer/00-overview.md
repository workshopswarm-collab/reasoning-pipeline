# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Soccer research should focus on squad strength, injuries, schedule congestion, league/tournament context, and the unusually large role of variance in low-scoring environments.

## Core conclusions

- Structure matters: league, cup, and tournament contexts differ.
- Small margins and finishing variance matter more than in many higher-scoring sports.
