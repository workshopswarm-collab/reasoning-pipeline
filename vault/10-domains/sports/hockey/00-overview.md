# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Hockey research should focus on star skater value, goaltending, roster depth, injuries, and how low-scoring variance interacts with playoff structure.

## Core conclusions

- Low scoring raises variance and makes goaltending/special teams crucial.
- Star players still matter, but depth and postseason randomness matter more than many casual observers think.
