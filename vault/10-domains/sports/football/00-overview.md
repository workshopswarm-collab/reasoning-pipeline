# Synthesis summary

Football research should focus on quarterback play, coaching, line quality, injuries, situational efficiency, and schedule/opponent context.

## Core conclusions

- Football is highly leverage-driven and role-sensitive.
- Injuries to key positions can dominate all other inputs.
- Coaching and scheme matter unusually much relative to many other sports.

## Main evidence clusters

- quarterback and offensive efficiency
- defense and line play
- injuries and availability
- coaching/system effects
- schedule and opponent strength
