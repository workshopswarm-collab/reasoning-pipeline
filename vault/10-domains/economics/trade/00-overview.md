# Synthesis summary

Trade research should focus on tariff/policy risk, supply chains, strategic dependence, and whether economic integration is deepening or fragmenting.

## Core conclusions

- Trade is both an economic and geopolitical system.
- Policy changes can reshape supply chains and market access quickly.
