# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Housing research should focus on rates, affordability, supply constraints, mortgage conditions, and how housing feeds back into politics and macro conditions.

## Core conclusions

- Housing is both a consumption good and a macro-sensitive asset.
- Rates and supply are the main recurring pressure points.
