The Oilers are a high-yield NHL entity because superstar concentration, contender status, and repeated title-market salience make them a major retrieval target.

An NHL franchise whose significance comes from elite star power and high-stakes championship expectations.

Edmonton matters because teams built around transcendent star talent create recurring questions about roster balance, playoff translation, and title viability.
