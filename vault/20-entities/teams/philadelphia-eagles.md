# Summary

The Eagles are a high-value football entity because strong roster construction, trench identity, and quarterback-dependent upside make them a recurring contender reference point.

## What this entity is

An NFL contender whose identity has recently combined roster depth, physicality, and Jalen Hurts-led offensive value.

## Why it matters

The Eagles matter because they are one of the clearest examples of how roster quality and quarterback play interact at contender level.

## Current state

Research should focus on Hurts' health and efficiency, line play, roster depth, coordinator/coaching stability, and whether the roster still supports title-level performance.

## Key strengths

- strong roster and trench baseline
- high upside when quarterback and system are aligned

## Key weaknesses

- offensive variance can shift quickly with health or play-calling changes
- contender status depends on sustaining roster edge, not just quarterback play

## Important recent changes

- remains one of the most important NFC contender entities

## Open questions

- how much structural roster edge persists as turnover and adaptation accumulate
