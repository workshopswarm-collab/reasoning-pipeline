# Summary

The Celtics profile as a deep contender whose ceiling is driven by star wing play plus roster balance.

## What this entity is

An NBA contender built around two-way versatility, lineup strength, and Tatum-centered star scalability.

## Why it matters

Boston is a canonical contender reference point: deep, talented, and structurally strong enough that small changes in health or shot variance matter a lot.

## Current state

Research should focus on playoff execution, shot-quality sustainability, and whether roster balance remains intact under health stress.

- deep roster quality
- strong two-way framework
- credible championship-level floor and ceiling

## Key weaknesses
