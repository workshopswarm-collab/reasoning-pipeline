# Summary

Denver remains one of the clearest star-system contenders in basketball, with team outlook highly concentrated around Nikola Jokić and the quality of supporting continuity.

## What this entity is

An NBA contender whose offensive identity and title viability are tightly linked to a singular superstar hub.

## Why it matters

Few teams are easier to read structurally: Jokić creates the ceiling, and support quality determines how much of that ceiling is realized.

## Current state

Research should focus on support-cast health, defense, and whether non-Jokić minutes remain stable enough in playoff settings.
