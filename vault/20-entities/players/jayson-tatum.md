# Summary

Jayson Tatum is the centerpiece of the Celtics' current contender identity and a scalable star whose value compounds with strong team depth.

## What this entity is

A two-way wing forward who provides high-level scoring, shot creation, and lineup flexibility.

## Why it matters

Boston evaluations should treat Tatum as the main bridge between elite roster depth and true championship ceiling.

## Current state

Research should focus on his efficiency in top-leverage contexts, co-star interaction, and how Boston's system amplifies or insulates his shot-making burden.

- scalable two-way profile
- high usage without collapsing lineup flexibility

## Key weaknesses
