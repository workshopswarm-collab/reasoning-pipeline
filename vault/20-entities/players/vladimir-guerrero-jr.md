Vladimir Guerrero Jr. is one of the Blue Jays' defining star bats and a major determinant of Toronto's offensive ceiling.

## What this entity is

A high-profile first baseman and middle-of-order offensive anchor with All-Star and award-level pedigree.

## Why it matters

Blue Jays evaluation meaningfully changes with Guerrero's production, health, and lineup support.

## Current state

Research should track his health, offensive form, surrounding lineup quality, and whether he is carrying or complementing team offense.

## Key weaknesses
