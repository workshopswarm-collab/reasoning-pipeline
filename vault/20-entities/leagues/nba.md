# Summary

The NBA is the central basketball league entity because title races, media narratives, player movement, and superstar economics are all structured through it.

## What this entity is

The premier professional basketball league in North America and the main reference league for global basketball markets.

## Why it matters

NBA context is necessary for interpreting contender strength, star-player value, playoff structure, and league-wide narrative swings.

## Current state

Research should track standings, playoff structure, injuries, transactions, and the league's media/broadcast environment.

## Key strengths

- highest basketball attention and information density
- strong historical/statistical infrastructure

## Key weaknesses

- narrative saturation can distort sober team-level evaluation
