# Summary

Tether is the most systemically important stablecoin entity in crypto because of its trading dominance, reserve questions, and role as global dollar-like market plumbing.

## What this entity is

A stablecoin issuer and token system centered on USDT, with very large market share and extensive exchange/deposit relevance.

## Why it matters

Tether is critical because many crypto markets effectively run on it. Confidence in Tether affects liquidity, settlement, and risk perception across the ecosystem.

## Current state

Research should track reserve transparency, regulatory treatment, chain distribution, market share, and whether confidence in redemptions remains stable.

## Key strengths

- dominant stablecoin market position
- deep integration across global crypto trading
- strong utility as settlement and liquidity instrument

## Key weaknesses

- reserve transparency skepticism remains a recurring issue
- concentration makes entity-specific risk ecosystem-relevant

## Important recent changes

- continued dominance in stablecoin share and trading relevance

## Open questions

- how robust the franchise is under tighter regulation
- whether dominance persists if transparency standards rise
