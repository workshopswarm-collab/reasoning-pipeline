# Summary

Russia is one of the most consequential geopolitical country entities because war, sanctions, energy, and great-power alignment questions repeatedly center on it.

## What this entity is

A nuclear-armed major power with large energy and military significance.

## Why it matters

Russia matters because conflict, sanctions, commodity risk, and alliance realignment often flow through its actions.

## Current state

Research should track war dynamics, sanctions adaptation, energy exports, domestic stability, and relationships with China, Europe, and the United States.
