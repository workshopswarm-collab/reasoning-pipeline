# Summary

Strategy (formerly MicroStrategy) is one of the most important corporate-Bitcoin entities because it turned treasury allocation into a core Bitcoin market narrative.

## What this entity is

A public software company that has become strongly identified with large-scale Bitcoin treasury accumulation.

## Why it matters

Strategy matters because it links public-equity behavior, leverage, and Bitcoin conviction into one of the clearest corporate-crypto feedback loops.

## Current state

Research should track BTC holdings, capital structure, issuance behavior, treasury strategy, and whether equity-market access amplifies Bitcoin exposure.

## Key strengths

- uniquely visible corporate-Bitcoin positioning
- strong narrative influence on treasury-adoption debates

## Key weaknesses

- extreme concentration around Bitcoin strategy creates entity-specific fragility
- leverage and capital-market conditions matter a lot

## Important recent changes

- rebranding and continued treasury emphasis deepen its identity as a Bitcoin proxy

## Open questions

- how sustainable the strategy is across different BTC and rates regimes
