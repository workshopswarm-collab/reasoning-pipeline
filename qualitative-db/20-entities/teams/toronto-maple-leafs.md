# Summary

The Maple Leafs are a high-yield NHL entity because market attention, fan intensity, and playoff-expectation volatility make them recurrently relevant.

## What this entity is

A globally visible NHL franchise whose significance comes from high attention, strong talent expectations, and repeated postseason scrutiny.

## Why it matters

Toronto matters because high-visibility hockey teams often produce persistent gaps between public expectation and actual playoff durability.

## Current state

Research should focus on contention level, playoff resilience, star dependence, injuries, and whether public pressure is exceeding structural team quality.

- massive fan and media attention make them one of the most market-facing NHL teams
- high-end offensive talent creates repeated pricing opportunities

## Key weaknesses

- playoff underperformance narratives can overwhelm clean current-state analysis
- public attention can produce persistent overpricing or overreaction

## Important recent changes
