Real Madrid is one of the highest-value soccer entities because historical prestige, Champions League pedigree, and elite squad expectations make it a perennial benchmark club.

Real Madrid matters because it is one of the clearest reference teams for championship pedigree, star-player concentration, and continental competition strength.
