# Summary

The Warriors remain one of the most system-defined teams in the league, but their current outlook still centers on how much Stephen Curry can bend defenses and how healthy the roster is around him.

## What this entity is

A veteran, style-driven NBA team built around movement shooting, offensive gravity, and playoff experience.

## Why it matters

Golden State remains a relevant contender/edge-case team because its system identity can outperform raw talent expectations when Curry is healthy.

## Current state

Research should focus on Curry availability, lineup competence, defensive stability, and whether age-related decline is being offset by system continuity.

## Key strengths

- unique offensive structure
- championship experience
- one of the greatest offensive engines ever at the center

## Key weaknesses

- health and age sensitivity
- narrower margin for error than in peak title years

## Important recent changes

- current news flow still reacts sharply to Curry and key injury updates

## Open questions

- whether system quality can still create deep-run upside around an aging core
