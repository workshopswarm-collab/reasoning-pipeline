Turkey is a high-value geopolitical entity because alliance balancing, regional conflict exposure, migration routes, and domestic political centralization all make it repeatedly important.

A strategically located state linking Europe, the Middle East, the Black Sea region, and NATO security architecture.

## Why it matters

Turkey matters because it sits at multiple geopolitical crossroads and often plays a balancing role between alliances, regional interests, and domestic politics.
