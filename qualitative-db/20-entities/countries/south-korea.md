# Summary

South Korea is a high-value prediction-market entity because it links peninsula security risk, semiconductor and industrial exposure, and alliance politics in Northeast Asia.

A major advanced economy and frontline U.S. ally facing persistent military and political pressure from North Korea while embedded in sensitive regional supply chains.

## Why it matters

South Korea matters because geopolitical, industrial, and electoral developments there can influence broader narratives about deterrence, trade, and chip supply resilience.

## Current state
