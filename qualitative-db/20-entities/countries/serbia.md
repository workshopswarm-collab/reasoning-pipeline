# Summary

Serbia is a high-yield entity because Balkan stability, EU friction, and balancing between Western institutions and Russia keep it strategically relevant.

## What this entity is

A Balkan state with significance in regional stability, European integration friction, and external balancing.

## Why it matters

Serbia matters because unresolved regional tensions and strategic ambiguity can produce disproportionate diplomatic and security significance.

## Current state

Research should focus on EU alignment, Russia relationship, regional tensions, domestic legitimacy, and whether balancing strategy is becoming more constrained.

- strong relevance to Balkan stability and EU periphery analysis
- can provide signal on how non-aligned European states respond to bloc pressure

## Key weaknesses
