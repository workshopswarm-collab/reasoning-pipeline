# Summary

Starknet is a high-yield crypto protocol because Ethereum scaling, validity-proof architectures, and next-generation rollup competition remain central ecosystem themes.

## What this entity is

A crypto scaling protocol associated with advanced proof-based infrastructure in the Ethereum-adjacent ecosystem.

Starknet matters because the long-run shape of Ethereum scaling may depend on whether technically differentiated rollup approaches can sustain developer and user adoption.
