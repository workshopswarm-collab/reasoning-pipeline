LINK is a useful token entity because it ties oracle-network relevance, infrastructure demand, and token-level market expectations into a distinct traded asset.

## What this entity is

A crypto asset associated with Chainlink, whose significance comes from oracle-network positioning, market recognition, and token-pricing sensitivity to infrastructure narratives.

## Why it matters

LINK matters because infrastructure tokens can trade on expectations about broad ecosystem dependence even when direct value-capture logic remains contested.

## Current state

Research should focus on oracle adoption, token utility, liquidity, ecosystem dependence, and whether infrastructure importance supports durable token demand.
