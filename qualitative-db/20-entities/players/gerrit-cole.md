# Summary

Gerrit Cole is one of the most important pitcher entities in baseball because frontline starter quality can disproportionately shape a contender's true ceiling.

## What this entity is

An elite MLB starting pitcher with Cy Young-level pedigree and high leverage over team outlook.

## Why it matters

Cole matters because Yankees evaluation becomes materially different when frontline rotation certainty changes.

## Current state

Research should track health, innings durability, strikeout/run-prevention baseline, and whether supporting rotation depth reduces dependence on him.

## Key strengths

- true ace-level upside and proven frontline value
- strong impact on playoff and regular-season team quality

## Key weaknesses

- pitcher health and durability introduce constant uncertainty
- heavy reliance on an ace can expose rotation fragility

## Important recent changes

- remains a core determinant of Yankee pitching strength and contender interpretation

## Open questions

- how much team resilience exists if Cole misses time or declines from peak form
