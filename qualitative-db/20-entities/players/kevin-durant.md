# Summary

Kevin Durant remains one of basketball's purest scoring stars, with team evaluation still sensitive to his efficiency, health, and integration into contender timelines.

## What this entity is

A veteran superstar forward with elite shotmaking across zones and proven championship pedigree.

## Why it matters

Durant can radically alter a team's offensive ceiling, but age and availability are now inseparable from his value.

## Current state
