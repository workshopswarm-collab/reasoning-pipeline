Juan Soto is one of baseball's highest-leverage offensive stars because elite on-base ability, power, and market visibility make him a major determinant of both true lineup strength and public expectation.

## What this entity is

A franchise-level corner bat with superstar offensive value and exceptional ability to shift team-level outlook.

## Why it matters

Soto matters because his presence can materially raise offensive floor and ceiling while also creating outsized narrative effects around whichever franchise he anchors.
