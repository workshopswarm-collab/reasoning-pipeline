# Summary

Copper is a high-yield crypto infrastructure entity because custody, settlement design, and institutional trading operations remain central bottlenecks in digital-asset market structure.

## What this entity is

A digital-asset infrastructure company focused on custody and institutional trading/settlement workflows.

## Why it matters

Copper matters because institutional crypto participation often depends on whether counterparties can reduce settlement and custody friction while preserving security and compliance.

## Current state
