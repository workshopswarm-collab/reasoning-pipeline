# Summary

Lambda is a high-yield AI infrastructure entity because GPU cloud access, AI compute supply, and developer/research workload deployment all run through its niche.

## What this entity is

A compute-infrastructure company focused on GPU cloud and AI workload support.

## Why it matters

Lambda matters because access to usable AI compute remains one of the main practical constraints on model development and deployment.
