Blackwell is a high-yield AI infrastructure product because next-generation accelerator performance, supply, and deployment cadence can materially affect AI-compute expectations.

A high-end AI compute product family associated with NVIDIA's next-stage accelerator roadmap.

Blackwell matters because frontier-model economics and AI-infrastructure expectations are highly sensitive to hardware generation transitions and availability.
