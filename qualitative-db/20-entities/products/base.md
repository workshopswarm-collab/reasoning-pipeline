# Summary

Base is a high-yield crypto product entity because it ties Coinbase distribution, Ethereum scaling, consumer onboarding, and onchain product strategy into one platform.

## What this entity is

A Coinbase-linked Ethereum layer-2 ecosystem product whose significance comes from connecting a major exchange to onchain applications and infrastructure.

## Why it matters

Base matters because it is one of the clearest test cases for whether large centralized platforms can successfully route mainstream users into onchain environments.

## Current state

Research should focus on user growth, app activity, fee and transaction patterns, Coinbase integration, and whether distribution advantage is translating into durable ecosystem strength.

## Key strengths

- strong distribution linkage via Coinbase
- direct relevance to mainstream onchain adoption questions
- useful bridge between centralized and decentralized crypto infrastructure

## Key weaknesses

- ecosystem quality can be overstated by launch hype or incentive-driven activity
- dependence on Coinbase distribution creates concentration risk

## Important recent changes

- remains one of the most important onchain platform experiments tied to a mainstream crypto brand

## Open questions

- whether distribution-led adoption will convert into durable usage and defensible ecosystem depth
