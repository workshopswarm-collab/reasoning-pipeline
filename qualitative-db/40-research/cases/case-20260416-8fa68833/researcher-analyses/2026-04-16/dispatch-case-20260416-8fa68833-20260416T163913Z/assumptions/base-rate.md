## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate above the market's midpoint and still clearly favoring Barcelona.
- Treating Barcelona as a deserved favorite rather than a coin-flip or only slight favorite.
- Interpreting recent results as reinforcing, rather than overturning, the broader team-strength prior.

- ESPN's 2026-04-22 scoreboard confirms the match is Barcelona home vs Celta Vigo.

- A venue change, postponement, or meaningful scheduling disruption.
- Fresh market/odds information showing Barcelona's win probability materially lower than current structural priors suggest.
