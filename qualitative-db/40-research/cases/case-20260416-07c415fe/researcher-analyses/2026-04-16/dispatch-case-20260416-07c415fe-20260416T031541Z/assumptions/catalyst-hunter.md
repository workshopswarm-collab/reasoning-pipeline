# Assumption

The main assumption is that no major crypto-wide risk-off shock or Solana-specific adverse event will hit before the Sunday 2026-04-19 12:00 ET Binance resolution candle.

## Why this assumption matters

SOL is already trading several dollars above the strike, so the forecast mostly depends on whether that cushion survives the next roughly three days rather than on a fresh bullish catalyst pushing price up from below 80.

## What this assumption supports

- A Yes probability modestly above the current market baseline.
- The view that absence of a negative catalyst is more important than arrival of a positive catalyst.
- The conclusion that the highest-information watch items are crypto-beta shocks, exchange/market-structure issues, or sharp weekend reversals.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Binance spot price was around 85.43 at retrieval.
- Recent daily closes and the last 72 hourly closes stayed above 80.
- BTC was also trading firm in the mid-70k area, reducing immediate cross-asset pressure on SOL.

## What would falsify it

- A sudden BTC-led market selloff that takes SOL back through 80.
- A Solana-specific outage, exploit, exchange disruption, or other operational shock that drives sharp repricing.
- A clear weekend loss of support with hourly closes clustering near or below 80 before Sunday noon ET.

## Early warning signs

- BTC rolling over sharply first.
- SOL losing the 82-83 zone and staying there.
- Weekend liquidity deterioration and accelerating downside volatility.
- Any exchange-specific irregularity affecting Binance SOL/USDT trading.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The case would shift from a high-probability hold-above-strike thesis to a much more coin-flip or No-leaning setup, because the contract resolves on a single timestamp rather than an average or range.
