# Summary

This is the coalition's official campaign site. It directly confirms that Progressive Bulgaria is an active election participant with a national program, named leadership, constituency leads, and active April 2026 campaign messaging.

## Key facts extracted

- The site presents Progressive Bulgaria as a live electoral project for Campaign 2026.
- It includes a full program, issue agenda, and a large slate of constituency leaders.
- Rumen Radev is presented prominently as the central public face of the coalition.
- News posts on the site reference 7 April 2026 campaign activity and election-day framing around 19 April.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- Progressive Bulgaria has public-facing campaign infrastructure and candidate organization.
- The coalition is contesting multiple constituencies rather than existing only as a paper registration.
- Its program emphasizes anti-oligarchy, state capacity, social justice, energy, AI/governance, and foreign policy themes.

## What is uncertain

- This source provides no neutral polling, vote-share, or seat-share evidence.
- Because it is self-published campaign material, it is not independent.
- It does not settle whether the coalition is ahead of GERB-SDS or other competitors.

## Why this source may matter

It is useful for extra verification that PB is a real and organized coalition with a visible nationwide campaign, reducing the chance that the market is mispricing a nonexistent or weakly formed entity.

## Possible impact on the question

This source supports the market's assumption that PB is a serious contestant. It does not support the stronger assumption that PB is almost certain to win the most seats.

## Reliability notes

High value for confirming existence, organization, and self-description; low value for relative electoral strength because it is partisan and non-independent.
