- PB is a **new entrant coalition** with no current parliamentary seats.
- GERB–SDS remains the largest current parliamentary bloc by a wide margin.
- The election system includes a threshold and proportional seat allocation, which creates friction for new entrants seeking an outright first-place finish.

## Why this source may matter

For an outside-view estimate, the most important fact is structural: PB appears to be a very new coalition with **0 incumbent seats**, competing against an already entrenched leading party. In fragmented parliamentary systems, new entrants can break through, but winning the most seats outright is usually much harder than merely entering parliament or disrupting rank order.
