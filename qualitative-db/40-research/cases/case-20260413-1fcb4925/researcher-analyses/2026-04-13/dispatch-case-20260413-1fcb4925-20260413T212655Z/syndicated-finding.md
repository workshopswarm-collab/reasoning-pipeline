# Claim

My post-synthesis view is that PB is more likely than not to finish first in seats, yet the remaining uncertainty around late polling quality, seat conversion, and reliance on partially indirect sourcing is too large for a near-lock price.

## Alpha summary

Market implies 95.95%; my syndicated range is 0.64 to 0.80. That still makes PB the favorite, but the edge is actionable only on the anti-overconfidence side, not as a strong anti-PB call. The likely market mispricing is treating a real frontrunner narrative around Radev/PB as if it were already terminally confirmed in seat terms.

## Why this could create or destroy alpha

This matters because the market appears to be pricing favorite-as-lock. If PB is merely a strong favorite in the 65-80% zone rather than a 96% near-certainty, that is a large pricing error in a date-sensitive event market. The alpha comes from distinguishing real directional strength from unjustified certainty. The alpha is destroyed if the market truly has access to stronger local evidence than was available here and that evidence already effectively settles the race.
