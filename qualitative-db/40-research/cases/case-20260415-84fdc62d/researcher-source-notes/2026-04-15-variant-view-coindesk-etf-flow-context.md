# Summary

CoinDesk reported that spot bitcoin ETFs took in $471 million on April 6, the strongest daily inflow since late February, while BTC still traded below 70k around April 7. The useful variant-view implication is that strong ETF demand was not producing an immediate clean breakout at that point, suggesting support is real but not invulnerable.

## Key facts extracted

- Spot bitcoin ETFs reportedly pulled in $471 million on April 6, described as the sixth-largest inflow day of 2026.
- CoinDesk described BTC trading around 68,780 on April 7 despite the inflows.
- The article argued that ETF demand was offsetting weak spot demand and large-holder distribution.
- It also cited Binance Research for the idea that ETF-driven flows may make BTC more forward-looking relative to expected policy changes.
