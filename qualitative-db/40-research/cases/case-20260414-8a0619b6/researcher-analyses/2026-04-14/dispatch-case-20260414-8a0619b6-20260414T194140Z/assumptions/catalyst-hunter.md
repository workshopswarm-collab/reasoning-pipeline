# Assumption

No major negative catalyst or exchange-specific dislocation will push Binance BTCUSDT down more than about 5.6% by the April 18 noon ET resolving minute.

## Why this assumption matters

The bullish case depends less on long-run Bitcoin conviction than on the short-run timing claim that the current cushion above 70k survives into one exact minute four days from now.

## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate above 50% and specifically above the market threshold being favored.
- The view that market pricing near 90% is somewhat rich but directionally reasonable.
- The claim that the most important catalyst is the arrival or non-arrival of a downside volatility shock before resolution.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Binance live spot during the run was about 74.1k, leaving more than 4k of cushion.
- No dominant scheduled bearish catalyst was verified in this run that obviously concentrates downside risk before April 18 noon ET.
- The contract resolves on a single minute close, so absent a sharp selloff or exchange-specific anomaly, current price context favors Yes.

## What would falsify it

- A macro, crypto-native, or exchange-specific event that drives BTC below 70k before or at the relevant minute.
- A sudden risk-off move large enough to erase the cushion.
- Evidence that the relevant Binance chart interpretation differs materially from the API-based time mapping used here.

## Early warning signs

- BTC losing 72k and failing to recover.
- Rising intraday realized volatility with repeated sharp downside moves.
- Exchange-specific operational issues on Binance near the resolution window.
- A material market repricing in adjacent threshold contracts.

## What changes if this assumption fails

If this assumption weakens, the main thesis should move quickly from modest skepticism toward the market's 90% to a more clearly bearish No-lean or at least a much lower Yes probability.
