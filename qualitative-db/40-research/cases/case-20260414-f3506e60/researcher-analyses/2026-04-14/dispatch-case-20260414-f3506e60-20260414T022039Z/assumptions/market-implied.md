# Assumption

DMK remains the most efficient seat-converting bloc in Tamil Nadu even if anti-incumbent votes are energized, because opposition votes are divided enough that DMK can still finish first in seats.

## Why this assumption matters

The market price only makes sense in the low-70s if DMK's incumbency and alliance structure still translate into seat efficiency rather than just statewide visibility. If anti-DMK votes consolidate effectively behind AIADMK or another challenger, the current price is too high.

## What this assumption supports

- A view that DMK should remain the favorite.
- A probability estimate close to, but slightly below, the market price.
- Interpretation that TVK is more likely to fragment the anti-incumbent field than to win the most seats itself.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- DMK enters from a strong 2021 seat baseline and incumbency.
- Available contextual reporting still frames AIADMK as the nearest alternative rather than showing a clear replacement frontrunner.
- TVK is new and seatless, which raises the hurdle for winning the most seats in its first assembly election.

## What would falsify it

- Credible late polling or broad constituency reporting showing AIADMK-led or other anti-DMK blocs clearly ahead in seat conversion.
- Strong evidence that TVK is not merely a spoiler but is actually converting broad vote share into a seat-winning map.
- Clear alliance consolidation that sharply reduces vote splitting against DMK.

## Early warning signs

- Repeated late-campaign reporting that DMK is losing swing constituencies across regions.
- Independent poll aggregation showing DMK under 40% of likely seats or no longer leading.
- Rapid market repricing toward AIADMK without a corresponding rumor-only explanation.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The current market-favoring interpretation would weaken quickly, and the correct estimate would move materially lower toward a toss-up or even below 50% depending on the scale of opposition consolidation.

- Main market-implied finding for this dispatch.
- Any later synthesis that treats the current Polymarket price as broadly efficient.
