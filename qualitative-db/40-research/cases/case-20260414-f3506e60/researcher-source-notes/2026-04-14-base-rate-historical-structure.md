# Summary

This source is useful for the structural outside view. It summarizes the most recent completed election and also notes the longer-running DMK/AIADMK duopoly in Tamil Nadu politics.

## Key facts extracted

- DMK won 133 of 234 seats in 2021; the DMK-led alliance won 159 seats.
- AIADMK won 66 seats in 2021.
- Tamil Nadu politics has been dominated by DMK and AIADMK for decades, with the page explicitly noting roughly 50 years of two-party regional dominance.
- The page summarizes the alternating pattern across major assembly elections since 1989, while also showing that 2021 was a decisive DMK victory.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- 2021 result: DMK 133 seats, AIADMK 66 seats.
- Alliance result: DMK-led alliance 159 seats versus AIADMK-led 75 seats.
