# Assumption

The most important near-term assumption is that no major negative crypto catalyst or exchange-specific shock emerges before the April 19 noon ET Binance candle, so current spot distance above $80 remains a meaningful cushion rather than a false comfort.

## Why this assumption matters

The bullish view is not based on a known scheduled upside catalyst; it is based on the combination of current price level, recent realized trading range, and the absence of an identified imminent negative trigger. If that calm-catalyst assumption is wrong, the market can reprice quickly despite starting above the strike.

## What this assumption supports

- A Yes probability materially above 50%
- A view that the market is directionally right to price Yes as favored
- A thesis that timing risk is mostly downside-event risk rather than lack of upside catalyst

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Binance spot at research time was 84.73, comfortably above the 80 strike.
- Recent Binance daily closes stayed above 80 for the most recent two-week sample pulled.
- The case window is short, only a few days until resolution, so persistence in regime matters more than long-term fundamental valuation.

## What would falsify it

- A broad crypto drawdown of roughly 6%+ in SOL before noon ET April 19

## Early warning signs
