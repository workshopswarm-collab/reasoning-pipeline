# Assumption

Absent a fresh macro or crypto-specific shock in the next six days, Binance BTC/USDT is likely to remain above 68,000 at the precise noon ET minute close on April 20.

## Why this assumption matters

The thesis depends less on long-run BTC direction and more on whether any near-term catalyst is strong enough to erase an ~8% buffer before a single settlement minute.

## What this assumption supports

- A high YES probability despite acknowledging event risk.
- The view that timing risk is mainly about downside shock arrival before Monday noon ET.
- The judgment that current market pricing is somewhat rich but not obviously irrational.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Current Binance spot is well above the strike.
- Recent Binance daily closes were also above the strike, suggesting the threshold is not only being cleared intraday.
- The contract window is short, so the burden of proof for NO is a fairly sharp and timely selloff rather than a slow drift.

## What would falsify it

- A material risk-off macro event, major crypto-specific negative headline, or exchange/liquidity disruption that pushes BTC toward or below 68k before settlement.
- A visible breakdown in BTC price structure over the next several sessions that compresses the cushion to near the strike.

## Early warning signs

- BTC losing the low-72k / low-70k area quickly.
- Weekend weakness or gap-down behavior that leaves little buffer by Sunday night.
- Any catalyst that sharply tightens financial conditions or hits crypto sentiment/liquidity.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The probability should move down quickly because this contract resolves on one exact minute, so once the cushion shrinks toward the strike, noise and microstructure matter much more.
