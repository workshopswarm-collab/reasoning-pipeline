# Assumption

BTC/USDT will likely remain in its current local trading regime over the next ~48 hours rather than experiencing a sharp drawdown large enough to push the April 17 12:00 ET Binance one-minute close below 72,000.

## Why this assumption matters

The base-rate thesis depends less on a bullish continuation and more on the threshold already being comfortably below spot. If current regime stability breaks, the market can still resolve No even from an above-threshold starting point.

## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate above 50%
- A view that the market’s high implied probability is directionally reasonable
- A conclusion that downside gap risk, rather than upside necessity, is the key mechanism

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

Recent Binance daily closes were above 72,000 and recent hourly prints were near 74,000-74,300. That means the market only needs BTC to avoid roughly a 3% downside move into the exact settlement minute, which is a modest but nontrivial condition for a 48-hour crypto horizon.

## What would falsify it

- A sharp macro or crypto-specific selloff before April 17 noon ET
- Binance-specific dislocation causing BTC/USDT on Binance to trade below broader-market levels
- A clear regime break with sustained trading under 72,000 before settlement

## Early warning signs

- BTC losing the 73k area and failing to reclaim it
- Elevated intraday volatility with repeated lower highs on Binance
- Exchange-specific operational disruptions or price dislocations

## What changes if this assumption fails

The estimate should move materially lower, likely toward a near-coinflip or below, because the whole outside-view case is currently anchored on existing cushion above the threshold.
