# Summary
Official ESL event page confirms IEM Rio 2026 is live, the event timing aligns with the market window, and Team Vitality is one of 16 invited teams. It also provides the official tournament structure that matters for title-risk analysis.

- Page title is "IEM Rio 2026 - ESL Pro Tour" and was modified on 2026-04-13.
- Tournament starts April 13, 2026; playoffs are April 17-19, 2026.
- 16 teams are listed.
- Format is two 8-team double-elimination groups, with top 3 from each group advancing.

- Team Vitality appears in the official team list as a Global VRS Invite.
- ESL page also labels Vitality as "2023 Champions," which is contextual color rather than direct evidence for 2026 win probability.
