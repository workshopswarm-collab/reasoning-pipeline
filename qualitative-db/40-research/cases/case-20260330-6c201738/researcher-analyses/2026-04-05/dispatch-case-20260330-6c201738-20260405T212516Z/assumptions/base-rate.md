Assumption note — base-rate

Core assumption: absent a later official Trump-administration action, the temporary Section 122 import surcharge described in official and contextual sources remains the operative general tariff layer relevant to the market on 2026-03-31 12:00 ET, and item-specific Section 232 / AD-CVD measures remain excluded by contract.

Why this matters:
- The market is not about effective tariff burden or product-specific duties.
- The market is about the general tariff rate in effect on a specific timestamp.
- If a later official action changes the general surcharge to above 15%, pauses it, expires it before the date, or replaces it with a different general China-relevant layer, the conclusion can change materially.

Fragility:
- Medium. The current view is robust only if no superseding official action occurs before the resolution date.
