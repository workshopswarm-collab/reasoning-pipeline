- A YES-leaning probability above the market's 0.78 implied baseline.
- The judgment that catalyst risk over the next six days is manageable rather than dominant.

- Binance spot check on 2026-04-15 shows BTCUSDT around 74,830, about 2,830 above threshold.

- A decisive move back under 73k with broad risk-off tone across equities and crypto.
