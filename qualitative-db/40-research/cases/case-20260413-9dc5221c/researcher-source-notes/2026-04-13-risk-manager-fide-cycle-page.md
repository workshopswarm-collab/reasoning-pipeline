# Summary

Official FIDE cycle page directly lists the 2026 Candidates Tournament field and format. It shows Javokhir Sindarov as qualified via the 2025 World Cup and states the 2026 Candidates is an 8-player double round-robin in Cyprus from March 28 to April 16, 2026, with a playoff if tied for first.

- FIDE names the 2026 Candidates Tournament dates and host: March 28-April 16, 2026, Cyprus.
- The winner of the event becomes the challenger for the world title.
- FIDE lists the eight qualifiers on the page.
- Javokhir Sindarov is listed as a qualifier via "2025 World Cup 1st".
- Other listed qualifiers are Fabiano Caruana, Wei Yi, Andrey Esipenko, Anish Giri, Matthias Bluebaum, Praggnanandhaa R, and Hikaru Nakamura.
- Ties for first are resolved by playoff, reducing resolution ambiguity around co-leaders.
