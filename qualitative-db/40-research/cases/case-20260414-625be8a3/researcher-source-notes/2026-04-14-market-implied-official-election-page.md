# Summary

This is the governing official source for the ballot question and the existence/timing of the April 21, 2026 special election.

## Key facts extracted

- The Virginia Department of Elections hosts the official page titled "Proposed Amendment for April 2026 Special Election."
- The page states the vote is for the "April 21, 2026, Special Election."
- Official ballot question: "Should the Constitution of Virginia be amended to allow the General Assembly to temporarily adopt new congressional districts to restore fairness in the upcoming elections, while ensuring Virginia's standard redistricting process resumes for all future redistricting after the 2030 census?"
- The page links official voter explanation and brochure PDFs.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- The referendum exists as an official statewide special-election ballot item.
- The ballot wording explicitly frames the change as temporary and tied to restoring fairness before reverting to the ordinary process after the 2030 census.

## What is uncertain

- The page does not itself indicate expected vote outcome.
- It does not itself resolve ongoing legal-challenge risk beyond showing the election is presently scheduled.
