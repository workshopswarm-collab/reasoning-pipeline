# Summary

This source is a useful contextual check on legal posture, election timing, and operational voting windows.

## Key facts extracted

- Early voting is underway and the special election is scheduled for April 21, 2026.
- WTOP reports the Virginia Supreme Court ruled March 4 that voters can cast ballots while legal challenges continue.
- WTOP also reports a Tazewell County circuit court had blocked the redistricting and that the supreme court's ultimate disposition could still affect whether the election result matters for map implementation.
