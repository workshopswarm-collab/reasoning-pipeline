# Assumption

BTC remaining roughly 5.7% above the $70,000 threshold on April 15 is the dominant state variable, and absent a meaningful negative shock it is more likely than not to still be above $70,000 at the specific April 20 noon ET close minute.

## Why this assumption matters

The final probability is mostly driven by current cushion versus threshold and the short remaining time window, not by any claim that Bitcoin cannot trade below $70,000 intraday before then.

## What this assumption supports

- A Yes probability materially above 50%.
- A view that the market is directionally right but slightly overconfident.
- The variant thesis that this is not a solved market despite current spot being well above threshold, because the contract is close-at-time-specific rather than touch-based.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Direct Binance spot checks show BTCUSDT around $73,995 on April 15.
- That leaves a buffer of roughly $3,995 above the contract threshold with about five days to go.
- Short-horizon BTC moves of that size are plausible but still not the modal path absent a clear negative catalyst.
- The contract mechanics focus on one exact minute close, which narrows the path dependence but does not eliminate ordinary downside volatility risk.

## What would falsify it

- A material BTC drawdown that takes price near or below $70,000 before the resolution window.
- New macro or crypto-specific stress that increases downside tail risk over the remaining days.
- Fresh evidence that the market’s noon ET close exposure is more fragile than the current buffer suggests.

## Early warning signs

- BTC quickly losing the current cushion and trading back toward the low $71k or $70k area.
- Elevated cross-market stress, funding deterioration, or abrupt risk-off headlines.
- Repeated inability to hold above $70k across liquid trading sessions.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The Yes case should be marked down materially, and the market’s high implied probability could become justified or even too low depending on how close price gets to $70k heading into April 20.
