# Assumption

The main assumption is that no major downside catalyst between now and Monday noon ET will push Binance BTC/USDT down more than roughly 4% from current spot and keep it below 72,000 at the exact resolution minute.

## Why this assumption matters

The bullish case is not that BTC is riskless; it is that the remaining catalyst window is short and current spot already sits meaningfully above the strike. If a large macro or crypto-specific shock lands before the noon ET print, the market can still flip despite today's favorable starting point.

## What this assumption supports

- A Yes-leaning probability above the market's 84.5% baseline.
- The view that path-to-repricing is mostly about whether any fresh negative catalyst appears before the weekend close / Sunday open / Monday morning window.
- The judgment that ordinary noise is less important than one discrete downside trigger in the remaining time window.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Binance spot was around 74.9k on April 16, leaving a cushion of roughly 2.9k above the strike.
- Recent daily candles show BTC has been spending time above 72k for several sessions.
- The contract horizon is very short: roughly four days to the relevant noon ET print.
- Because crypto trades continuously, there is no earnings-style single scheduled catalyst required for a repricing; absent a negative shock, spot drift and existing level advantage favor Yes.

## What would falsify it

- A sharp risk-off macro event or crypto-specific negative shock that pushes BTC/USDT back below 72k before Monday noon ET.
- Exchange-specific dysfunction on Binance that creates an anomalous BTC/USDT print around the resolution minute.
- Evidence that the market is misreading the candle-time interpretation and the operative minute is not the one assumed.

## Early warning signs

- BTC loses the 74k area and fails to reclaim it.
- Weekend price action shows accelerating downside momentum toward 72k rather than range stability.
- Cross-venue weakness begins to concentrate specifically on Binance BTC/USDT.
- New regulatory, exchange, or geopolitical headlines trigger broad crypto deleveraging.

## What changes if this assumption fails

If a downside catalyst emerges or Binance-specific execution risk rises, the case should move from modest-over-Yes to much closer to the market or even below it, because the remaining buffer over 72k is not enormous in BTC terms.
