- USA Today reports that Forbes listed Elon Musk at **$342 billion** net worth in its 2025 annual billionaire ranking.
- The article says Forbes calculated the list using **March 7 stock prices and exchange rates**.
- The article explicitly attributes Musk's top ranking to his positions tied to Tesla and SpaceX, while also noting ownership stakes in X and roles with other ventures.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- Musk was first on Forbes' 2025 billionaire list at $342 billion.
