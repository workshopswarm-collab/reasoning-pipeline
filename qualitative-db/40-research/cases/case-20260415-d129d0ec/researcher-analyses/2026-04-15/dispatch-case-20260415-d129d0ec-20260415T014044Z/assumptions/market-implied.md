# Assumption

The market's 0.73 price is implicitly assuming that Russia's current nationwide aerial attack tempo will translate into at least one drone or missile strike directed against Kyiv municipality before the April 17 EET deadline.

## Why this assumption matters

Without that translation from broad Ukraine strike activity to Kyiv-specific targeting, a high Yes price is too aggressive because the contract is narrow and location-specific.

## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate above 50%.
- A view that the market is directionally reasonable rather than obviously stale.
- A thesis that current public information justifies a strong prior for another Kyiv-directed wave.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Large overnight Russian drone/missile waves remain active as of April 14.
- Kyiv is a recurrent, high-salience target in Russian long-range strike campaigns.
- Zelenskyy publicly warned of another likely drone/missile attack the night of April 14-15.
- The contract counts intercepted attacks if clearly directed at Kyiv municipality, lowering the bar versus requiring successful impact on the ground.

## What would falsify it

- One or two further nationwide strike waves occur before deadline with no credible indication that Kyiv municipality was targeted.
- Ukrainian official and media reporting specifically emphasize other oblasts while Kyiv remains absent through the window.
- Weather, operational, or targeting choices shift Russian attacks away from Kyiv during the relevant period.

## Early warning signs

- Repeated public reporting of strikes concentrated in southern or eastern oblasts only.
- Kyiv alerts without subsequent evidence of incoming drones/missiles directed at the municipality.
- Official overnight summaries that mention no Kyiv-targeted activity.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The market would look overstretched; the probability should move materially below the current 0.73 because the contract is not "Russia attacks somewhere in Ukraine" but specifically Kyiv municipality.
