# Summary

BBC is the strongest accessible source for the yes case because it reports actual Iranian drone impacts on UAE territory, not just interceptions. For this contract, that distinction is decisive.

## Key facts extracted

- On 16 March 2026 BBC reported that Fujairah and Dubai airport had come under further drone attacks as Iran continued to target Gulf transport and oil infrastructure.
- BBC says a fire broke out near Dubai airport after a "drone-related incident".
- BBC says a drone attack on the port and industrial zone at Fujairah caused a fire.
- BBC also reports that a drone hit an oil storage tanker on Saturday and another drone hit oil facilities on Monday.
- In the same piece, the UAE Defence Ministry said air defences intercepted six ballistic missiles and 21 drones on Monday.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- Direct territorial effects in the UAE are described: fires near Dubai airport and at Fujairah oil infrastructure.
- The article frames these as Iranian drone attacks, not merely air-defence engagements.
