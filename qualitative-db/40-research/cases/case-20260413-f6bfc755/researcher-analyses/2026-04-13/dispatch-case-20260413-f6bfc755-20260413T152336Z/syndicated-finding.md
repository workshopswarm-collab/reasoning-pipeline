# Claim

Thrash is still more likely than not to finish as the top US Netflix movie for the relevant unpublished 4/6/26-4/12/26 week, but the 0.90 market price appears too confident because the governing Netflix chart had not yet published and the bundle does not independently verify the large market-confidence gap strongly enough.

Market implies 0.90; my post-synthesis range is 0.62-0.80. The synthesis therefore identifies potential overconfidence, but not a clean, highly verified anti-market edge.
