## Key facts extracted

- The next Bulgarian parliamentary election is described as scheduled for 19 April 2026.
- In the current National Assembly ordering shown on the page, GERB-SDS has 66 seats, PP-DB 36, Revival 33, and DPS 29.
- That current seat ordering makes GERB-SDS the incumbent largest bloc, not the second largest.

- The electoral system is proportional representation with a 4% threshold, which raises coalition/fragmentation risk and means second place can be sensitive to modest vote-share moves among several mid-sized blocs.

## Evidence directly stated by source

## What is uncertain

- This is not an official election authority.
- Polling data were not clearly surfaced in the fetched extract, so it mostly informs field structure and incumbent ordering rather than vote-intention trends.
- Because Bulgarian politics is highly fluid, current-seat ordering is only indirect evidence for the upcoming election result.

## Why this source may matter

## Possible impact on the question
