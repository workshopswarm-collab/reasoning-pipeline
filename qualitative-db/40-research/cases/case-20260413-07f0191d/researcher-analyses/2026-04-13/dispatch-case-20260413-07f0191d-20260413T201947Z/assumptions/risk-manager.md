# Assumption

The market appears to assume GERB-SDS is much more likely to land in a narrow "second place" slot than to finish either first or below second.

## Why this assumption matters

The contract resolves on exact rank, not broad competitiveness. If GERB-SDS is simply very likely to finish top-two, that is not enough to justify a very high probability on finishing exactly second.

## What this assumption supports

- A near-certain market price around 0.96.
- The idea that one rival is very likely to overtake GERB-SDS while all other rivals are very unlikely to do so.
- The interpretation that current uncertainty is mostly about who wins first, not whether GERB-SDS moves across multiple rank positions.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- GERB-SDS is a major established coalition and a central pole of Bulgarian politics.
- A crowded field can create a common heuristic that the largest incumbent bloc may settle into second if anti-incumbent energy consolidates behind one challenger.
- Recent Bulgarian electoral volatility may tempt traders to compress a nuanced rank distribution into a single most-salient scenario.

## What would falsify it

- Fresh independent polling showing GERB-SDS clearly first rather than second.
- Fresh independent polling showing GERB-SDS in a close cluster with PP-DB and Revival where third or lower is plausible.
- Official or high-quality reporting indicating a coalition rupture, candidate shock, or campaign event that materially alters GERB-SDS vote efficiency.

## Early warning signs

- Inconsistent reporting on which bloc is actually ahead nationally.
- Polls with wide spreads across fieldwork dates or pollsters.
- Evidence that Revival or another challenger is consolidating protest votes faster than expected.
- Ambiguity over whether market participants are confusing top-two probability with second-place probability.

## What changes if this assumption fails

If exact-rank confidence is overstated, the fair probability for GERB-SDS finishing second should fall materially below the market price, even if GERB-SDS remains one of the most likely top-two finishers.
