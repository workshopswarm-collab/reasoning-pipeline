# Decision summary

GERB-SDS is plausibly the likeliest exact second-place finisher only under a specific PB-first/GERB-second race structure, but the independently checked public evidence does not verify that structure strongly enough to support either the market’s 0.96 price or the strongest yes-leaning lane. My post-synthesis view is that GERB-SDS finishing second is possible and maybe modestly more likely than not, but still far from locked.

## Why this may matter now

Market implies 0.96; my synthesized range is 0.42-0.68. That is below market and not a clean actionable edge on the available verification standard because the apparent mispricing is large but only partially independently verified. The likely market error, if any, is overconfidence in an exact-rank outcome without sufficiently verified proof that GERB has already shifted from first-place contender into durable runner-up.

## Shift versus swarm baseline

The provisional swarm center was 0.58. My final range is centered near that baseline rather than near either tail. I moved away from the most bearish lanes because fresh synthesis checks did verify that Progressive Bulgaria is at least present in accessible public election context and that a PB-first/GERB-second configuration is not imaginary. But I also moved far away from the variant-view high-confidence yes case because that mechanism was not independently verified strongly enough to justify near-certainty.

## Edge verification status

Independent verification was partial. I independently rechecked accessible external sources and confirmed: the contract mechanics are clear; the election date is 19 April 2026; GERB-SDS remains a top-tier force in accessible POLITICO context; and Wikipedia context shows Progressive Bulgaria on the ballot, reducing the chance that the variant lane invented a wholly nonexistent competitor. But I did not independently verify a robust domestic polling consensus that GERB is now clearly second behind exactly one rival. That keeps verification quality at medium rather than high.

## Compression toward market

Yes. The swarm contained a very large below-market edge and one strong near-market yes case. Because the market-vs-swarm gap was so large, I required stronger independent proof than the bundle supplied. I therefore compressed the final range toward the market relative to the most bearish lanes, but not all the way, because verification did not support a 0.96 exact-second probability either.

## Timing and catalyst posture

The key catalyst is final-week polling and then election-night seat-based projections. The edge is more likely to compress rapidly once credible local polling or seat projections clarify whether GERB is first or second. Waiting for higher-quality late evidence would likely improve decision quality more than elaborating current arguments further.

## Key blockers

Main blockers: lack of strong independent late-cycle Bulgarian polling confirmation; incomplete official-source verification due to CIK access problems; and unresolved race-structure disagreement over whether GERB is still mostly a first-place contender or has shifted into a stable second-place slot. Those are real blockers for a high-confidence decision.

## Best countercase

Best countercase: GERB-SDS is still more likely first than second, and the market is badly overpricing exact second because traders are anchoring on a mislabeled or weakly verified race structure. Base-rate and market-implied represented this countercase best.

## What would change the view

I would move materially upward if multiple independent late Bulgarian polls or election-night seat projections showed one bloc clearly first and GERB clearly second. I would move materially downward if credible late polling showed GERB still leading, or if PB taxonomy/ballot interpretation proved overstated or invalid for rank ordering.

## Recommended next action

Collect one stronger domestic late-poll or election-reporting source, then request decision-maker review if the market still prices near certainty. If no better source is available, wait for the next catalyst rather than overcommitting to a large edge.
