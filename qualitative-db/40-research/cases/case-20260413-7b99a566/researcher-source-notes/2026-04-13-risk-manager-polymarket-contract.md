# Summary

The market resolves Yes only if a diplomatic meeting between representatives of Israel and Lebanon occurs by April 19, 2026, 11:59 PM ET, with strict conditions on official capacity, deliberate diplomatic purpose, in-person presence, and public acknowledgment or consensus credible reporting.

## Key facts extracted

- The meeting must involve representatives acting in an official capacity and authorized to engage in diplomacy regarding Israel-Lebanon relations.
- Indirect meetings through mediators or facilitators can count, but they must still be in-person.
- Brief greetings, chance encounters, and non-diplomatic interactions do not count.
- Remote meetings and phone calls do not count.
- Resolution sources are official information from the governments of Israel and Lebanon and a consensus of credible reporting.
- Deadline is April 19, 2026, 11:59 PM ET.

- The contract directly defines what counts and what does not count.
