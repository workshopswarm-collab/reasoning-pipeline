# Assumption

The current public signals about direct negotiations and an imminent Lebanese–Israeli meeting will convert into a contract-qualifying in-person diplomatic meeting that is publicly acknowledged or credibly confirmed before April 19, 2026 11:59 PM ET.

## Why this assumption matters

The market is not asking whether negotiations exist in the abstract; it asks whether a narrowly defined meeting happens and can be verified under the contract. Most of the bullish case depends on this conversion step.

## What this assumption supports

- Any estimate materially above 50%
- The interpretation that current media and official rhetoric are actionable rather than merely atmospheric
- The view that current diplomatic architecture is mature enough to produce a visible meeting within days

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Lebanese media are discussing an apparent upcoming Lebanese–Israeli meeting rather than only general diplomacy.
- Naharnet reports Lebanese Foreign Minister Rajji publicly describing direct negotiations with Israel.
- The short contract window can favor markets if a meeting is already being organized.

## What would falsify it

- Credible reporting that the expected meeting was postponed, canceled, or never existed
- Evidence that contacts remain indirect/remote only
- Failure to produce any official acknowledgment or consensus credible reporting by the deadline
- Evidence that the discussed contact is military/technical deconfliction or a chance encounter that fails the contract wording

## Early warning signs

- Continued reports about negotiations with no named meeting, venue, or authorized participants
- Heavy fighting that makes hosting or attendance impractical
- Reports emphasizing mediation without physical co-presence
- Divergence between Lebanese and Israeli public accounts

## What changes if this assumption fails

The case should move materially toward No because the current high market price appears to rely on near-term execution rather than a long runway for diplomacy.
