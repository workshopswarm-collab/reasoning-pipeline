# Assumption

The announced or expected Israel-Lebanon talks will either occur in a contract-qualifying in-person format before April 19 or fail in a way that remains visible enough for consensus reporting.

## Why this assumption matters

The probability estimate depends less on whether some diplomatic process exists in principle and more on whether it converts into a clearly qualifying and publicly confirmable meeting before the deadline.

## What this assumption supports

- A probability materially above a cold base rate.
- A view that the market is directionally grounded in real near-term diplomacy.
- A discount versus the market because execution and wording risk remain meaningful.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

Multiple recent reports indicate talks were approved, expected, or being hosted soon. That means the pathway is live. But in active conflict settings, planned talks often slip, narrow, or become ambiguous in form. The contract’s in-person and public-acknowledgment requirements create an additional conversion hurdle from “talks are in motion” to “Yes resolves.”

## What would falsify it

- A clear official or consensus-media report that talks were postponed, canceled, or shifted to remote-only form.
- A report showing the contact was only through mediators without the relevant in-person presence required by the contract.
- A report showing only informal or military deconfliction contacts rather than diplomacy by authorized official representatives.

## Early warning signs

- Continued or intensified cross-border strikes crowding out the reported meeting window.
- Media language shifting from “will be held” to “may be held” or “under discussion.”
- Reporting that the participants are technical/security officials without a clearly diplomatic mandate.
- No public confirmation as the deadline approaches.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The probability should move down sharply, because the short-dated market gives little time to recover from one slip in scheduling or one failure to satisfy the wording cleanly.
