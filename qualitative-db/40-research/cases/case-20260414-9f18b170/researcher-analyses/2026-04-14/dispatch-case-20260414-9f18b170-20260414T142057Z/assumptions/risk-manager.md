# Assumption

If BTC is already trading in the mid-$75k range across major spot references, Binance BTC/USDT is likely to print at least one 1-minute High at or above $76,000 before the April 19 ET cutoff.

## Why this assumption matters

The final view depends less on broad weekly BTC direction than on whether a small remaining gap is crossed on the exact settlement venue and time definition.

## What this assumption supports

- A high Yes probability rather than merely a modest lean.
- The interpretation that most residual risk is exchange-specific path risk rather than macro thesis failure.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Independent spot references cluster around $75.7k at research time.
- Weekly threshold markets are often reached by transient wick behavior, not only sustained closes.
- Several lower threshold ladder markets on the same Polymarket page had already resolved Yes, showing realized upside path in the same weekly window.

## What would falsify it

- Binance BTC/USDT remains persistently below $76,000 while other venues briefly trade above it.
- A sharp BTC reversal takes spot materially away from the threshold for the remainder of the window.
- Exchange-specific dislocation or unusual basis keeps Binance highs under the line despite broad-market strength.

## Early warning signs

- Binance trading at a persistent discount to Coinbase/Kraken.
- Spot BTC loses momentum and falls back toward low-$74k or below.
- Liquidity deteriorates enough that upside continuation stalls after the initial squeeze.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The high-conviction Yes view becomes too aggressive; probability should move meaningfully lower and the market’s current extreme confidence would look overstated.
