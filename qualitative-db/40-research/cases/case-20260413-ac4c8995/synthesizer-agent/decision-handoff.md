# Decision summary

United Left (BSP) is still more likely than not to win at least one seat in the 2026 Bulgarian parliamentary election, but the best post-synthesis view is only modestly yes-lean and slightly below the market because the core positive case is mostly a structural threshold-survival argument while the key missing verification remains fresh independent evidence that BSP–United Left is safely above the 4% threshold right before the vote.

## Why this may matter now

Market-implied probability is 0.735. My syndicated range is 0.66 to 0.74. That makes the edge versus market marginal to negative rather than actionable. The most plausible reason the market could still be a bit rich is that the bullish case is driven more by continuity and prior representation than by well-verified current threshold safety; if there is no fresh sub-threshold evidence, Yes remains the base case, but the range is not wide enough to support a strong contrarian view.

## Shift versus swarm baseline

This is only slightly above the provisional swarm center near 0.68. The reason for not staying at the exact center is that synthesis-stage checking did independently reinforce several structural supports: election date, threshold mechanics, existing parliamentary status, and contract fallback to CIK. The reason for not moving up toward the most bullish personas is that the extra verification still did not close the biggest gap: fresh independent evidence that BSP–United Left is safely above 4% now.

## Edge verification status

Independent verification quality is medium. The synthesis pass independently checked and confirmed: the 19 April 2026 election date, the 4% threshold structure, that BSP–United Left is represented as a current parliamentary coalition with 19 seats in accessible contextual sources, and that the contract explicitly uses credible reporting with CIK fallback. That matters because it validates the core mechanism: this is a threshold-survival seat-entry market. What remains weak is the most important marginal question for pricing: current vote share relative to threshold and clean official registration/entity mapping. Because those were not directly resolved, the final edge versus market is only moderately verified at best.

## Compression toward market

No. The synthesis did not compress toward the market because the swarm baseline was already below market, not above it. If anything, the synthesis made only a mild upward adjustment from the swarm center after confirming the structural supports. But it refused to follow the more bullish lanes because the missing late-cycle verification remained too important.

## Timing and catalyst posture

The key catalyst is final pre-election information: reputable late polling, local reporting on threshold safety, or official ballot/entity clarification from CIK. The edge is more likely to compress toward a sharper answer once one of those appears. Waiting could improve decision quality materially because this is a high-freshness threshold market and a single credible late poll could move the estimate several points.

## Key blockers

The main blockers are: missing strong fresh independent polling; no direct CIK verification of ballot/entity naming; and the nonlinear nature of threshold markets. There is not a severe contract blocker, but there is enough unresolved factual/timing uncertainty to prevent high-confidence action.

## Best countercase

The strongest countercase, best represented by risk-manager and market-implied, is that this market is overpriced because a stale 2024 baseline is doing too much work in a fragmented snap-election environment; if BSP–United Left is now hovering near 4% or if the official ballot mapping is messy, zero seats becomes very live.

## What would change the view

A credible late poll or polling average showing BSP–United Left below 4% would push the estimate down materially and could make No favored. Direct CIK confirmation of clean ballot registration plus multiple polls safely above threshold would push the estimate up. Credible reporting of coalition rupture, withdrawal, or entity-label mismatch would also move the view down sharply.

## Recommended next action

Collect one or two high-quality late local polling or reporting sources on BSP–United Left's threshold position, plus direct official ballot/entity confirmation if available. If that cannot be obtained, treat current pricing as roughly efficient to slightly rich on Yes rather than a strong opportunity.
