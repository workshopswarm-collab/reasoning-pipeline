# Summary

Official enacted-law text confirms that the Reforming Intelligence and Securing America Act became law on April 20, 2024 as Public Law 118-49. This matters because the market description explicitly says qualifying legislation includes Public Law 118-49.

## Key facts extracted

- Public Law 118-49 is the Reforming Intelligence and Securing America Act.
- It was enacted on April 20, 2024.
- The law amends Section 702 of FISA, codified at 50 U.S.C. 1881a.
- This is the strongest available source for whether qualifying legislation already exists.

## Evidence directly stated by source

- The page labels the act as "Public Law 118-49" and dates it "Apr. 20, 2024 - [H.R. 7888]".
- The act repeatedly references section 702 of FISA (50 U.S.C. 1881a).

## What is uncertain

- The govinfo law text alone does not cleanly surface, in the excerpt reviewed, the operative sunset date after amendment; that required a second verification pass via enrolled bill text and current codification.

## Why this source may matter

This is primary-source proof that a qualifying law exists and was already enacted well before the market deadline.

## Possible impact on the question

If the market accepts Public Law 118-49 as qualifying legislation, then the key remaining question is whether that law in fact reauthorized Title VII/Section 702 before expiration. Combined with the codified statute showing the updated sunset, this is strongly supportive of Yes.
