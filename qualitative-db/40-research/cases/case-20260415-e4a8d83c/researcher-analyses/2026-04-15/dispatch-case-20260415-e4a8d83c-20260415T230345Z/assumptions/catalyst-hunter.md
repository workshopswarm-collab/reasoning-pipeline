# Assumption

The most important assumption is that no new macro or crypto-specific shock before Friday noon ET will push Binance BTC/USDT back below 74,000 at the exact settlement minute.

## Why this assumption matters

The thesis is not that Bitcoin is structurally bullish over weeks; it is that current price cushion and ordinary path dependence are enough to keep BTC above the threshold at one specific minute.

## What this assumption supports

- A modest Yes lean rather than a near-certain Yes.
- The view that current above-threshold spot is informative but not decisive.
- The judgment that catalyst risk over the next ~41 hours matters more than long-run Bitcoin fundamentals.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Current Binance spot is above 74,000.
- Recent 24h trading range also spent time above 74,000 for substantial stretches.
- The market-implied probability around 71.5%-72% already embeds some but not total reversal risk.

## What would falsify it

- A sharp risk-off macro move, exchange-specific disruption, or crypto headline that quickly pushes BTC/USDT into a lower trading regime.
- Sustained trading below 74,000 late on April 16 or early on April 17.

## Early warning signs

- Loss of 74,000 on Binance with follow-through rather than a brief wick.
- Expansion in downside intraday range toward or below the recent 24h low regime.
- A repricing in adjacent Polymarket ladders toward 74k being near-even or worse.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The case would move from modest Yes lean to toss-up or No lean, because this contract is dominated by timing and threshold fragility rather than a broad long-term directional edge.
