# Summary
Official Netflix Tudum reporting shows `War Machine` debuted at #1 on the English film list for the week of March 2-8 with 39.3 million views, while `Jurassic World Rebirth` was already far behind at #2 with 6.7 million views. A secondary March 15 analysis from What's on Netflix says `War Machine` rose to 83.7 million cumulative views after two weeks and had overtaken `The Rip` as Netflix's biggest movie of 2026 so far.

## Key facts extracted
- Netflix Tudum reported `War Machine` debuted at #1 on the English film list across 93 countries with 39.3 million views for the week of March 2-8, 2026.
- In the same official recap, `Jurassic World Rebirth` ranked #2 with 6.7 million views.
- What's on Netflix reported that for the week of March 9-15, `War Machine` had a near 13% rise in viewing hours and reached 83.7 million cumulative views after two weeks.
- What's on Netflix also characterized `War Machine` as Netflix's most-watched movie of 2026 so far by that point.
- What's on Netflix noted `The Rip` opened slightly higher at 41.6 million views but ultimately trailed `War Machine` on two-week cumulative views.

## Evidence directly stated by source
- Netflix Tudum: `War Machine` was No. 1 on the English film list with 39.3 million views.
- Netflix Tudum: `Jurassic World Rebirth` was No. 2 with 6.7 million views in that same week.
- What's on Netflix: `War Machine` reached 83.7 million cumulative views after two weeks.
- What's on Netflix: `War Machine` surpassed `The Rip` as the most-watched Netflix movie of 2026 so far.
