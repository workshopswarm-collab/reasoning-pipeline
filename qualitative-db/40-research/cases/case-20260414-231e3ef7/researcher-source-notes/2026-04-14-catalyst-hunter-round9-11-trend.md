# Summary

This narrows the remaining live catalyst set by round 12 to essentially Giri plus residual mathematical long shots.

- Round 9: FIDE wrote that Sindarov missed a chance to beat Bluebaum, while Giri beat Caruana; “with five rounds remaining, Sindarov still leads by 1.5 points.”
- Round 10: FIDE wrote Sindarov beat Praggnanandhaa and “now leads the field by a full two points” with four rounds left.
- Round 11: FIDE wrote Sindarov “still holds a two-point lead” after drawing Caruana, with three rounds remaining.
- Round 11 article explicitly highlights Caruana's missed opportunity to dent the lead and Giri's inability to create winning chances against Esipenko.
