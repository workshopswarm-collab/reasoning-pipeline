If anything, verification limits mainly prevented moving upward toward the market.

This is highly timing-sensitive. The next material checkpoint is official FIDE reporting after round 13; round 14 or formal winner declaration is the decisive endpoint. The edge is more likely to decay than widen if favorable official results arrive for Sindarov, because residual uncertainty collapses fast late in tournament markets.
