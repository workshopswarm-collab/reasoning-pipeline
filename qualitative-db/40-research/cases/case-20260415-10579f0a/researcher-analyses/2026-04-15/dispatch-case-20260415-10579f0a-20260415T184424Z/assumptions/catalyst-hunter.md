The thesis that Yes remains highly likely depends less on long-run Bitcoin fundamentals than on the absence of a near-term shock before a very specific intraday settlement minute.

## What this assumption supports

- A high Yes probability despite not being 100%
- The view that market pricing near 96.5-97% is directionally justified
