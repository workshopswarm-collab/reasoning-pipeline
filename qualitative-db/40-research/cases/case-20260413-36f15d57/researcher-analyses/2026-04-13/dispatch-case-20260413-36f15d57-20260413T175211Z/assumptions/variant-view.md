## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate below the 70% market-implied level.

- DeepSeek's currently checked public surfaces remain centered on V3.2.

- Homepage or platform text shifting from V3.2 to a successor V release.
- A public model page, repo, or signup surface for a next-major V model.

## What changes if this assumption fails
