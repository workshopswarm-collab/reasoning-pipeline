This note checks whether a major scheduled U.S. macro catalyst sits inside the remaining window before the April 20 noon ET settlement observation.

- Federal Reserve 2026 calendar shows the next FOMC meeting on April 28-29, after the market resolves.

## Evidence directly stated by source

## What is uncertain

## Why this source may matter

For a catalyst-hunter view, the absence of a major scheduled macro event is itself useful. It suggests that the path to failure for the 72k threshold is more likely to come from unscheduled risk-off shocks or ordinary BTC volatility than from a known calendar event with obvious repricing power.

## Possible impact on the question

The market may be correctly discounting a relatively quiet scheduled macro window into April 20. That supports a modestly bullish / threshold-holding view, while keeping attention on weekend volatility and unscheduled risk events as the main threat.
