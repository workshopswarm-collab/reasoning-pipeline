The key next checkpoint is official NHL final scoring/award confirmation. Before that checkpoint, the edge is more likely to decay than widen because this is already a mature market and most uncertainty is about final confirmation rather than new substantive information. Waiting is only helpful if it delivers clean official confirmation; otherwise it mostly burns residual edge.

The remaining blockers are modest but real: no clean directly archived official NHL winner page in the bundle, minor wording ambiguity from the finalist clause, and a small unresolved tail around final completion/stat correction status. There is no major factual blocker on who appears to be leading; the blockers are mainly verification-quality blockers.
