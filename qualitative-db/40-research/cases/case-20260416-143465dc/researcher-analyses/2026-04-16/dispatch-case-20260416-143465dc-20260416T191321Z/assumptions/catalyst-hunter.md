# Assumption

A continued broadly supportive crypto tape over the next several sessions is sufficient to generate at least one Binance 1-minute SOL/USDT wick from the current high-88 area through $90, even without a fresh Solana-specific headline.

## Why this assumption matters

The current thesis is not that SOL must trend sustainably above 90 by the weekend close. It is that the market only needs one qualifying intraminute spike, so general beta follow-through and volatility can do most of the work.

## What this assumption supports

- A probability estimate above a pure coin-flip for a $90 touch.
- The judgment that timing and path matter more than terminal weekly close.
- The view that the most important catalyst may be continuation of a broad crypto risk-on move rather than a named Solana event.

## Evidence or logic behind the assumption

- Binance data shows SOL already reached 89.15 during the eligible window, leaving less than 1% additional upside needed.
- The 24h move was already positive and large enough to show active momentum.
- Broader crypto reporting suggests a supportive but fragile environment, which is consistent with brief breakout attempts and wick risk.
- Touch markets are structurally easier to satisfy than close-above markets when the asset is already near the threshold.

## What would falsify it

- A clear reversal in the broader crypto tape that pushes SOL materially away from 90.
- Repeated rejection near 89-89.2 with declining momentum and no renewed volatility.
- New market stress that makes near-term high-beta follow-through less likely.

## Early warning signs

- Bitcoin continues failing at resistance and drags high-beta alts lower.
- SOL loses the high-88 handle and trades back into the mid-86 to 87 area.
- Intraday volatility compresses instead of expanding.

## What changes if this assumption fails

The probability of a $90 touch should be marked down materially because the thesis is carrying little cushion; without continued tape support, there may be no remaining meaningful catalyst before April 19.
