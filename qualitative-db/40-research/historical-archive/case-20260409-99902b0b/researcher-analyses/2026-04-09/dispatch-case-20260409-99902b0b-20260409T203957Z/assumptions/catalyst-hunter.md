## What this assumption supports

- A high but not near-certain Yes probability.
- A view that the strongest catalyst is simply the absence of a sharp overnight selloff.
- A judgment that the market is directionally right but somewhat too confident at the extreme.

- Binance spot was ~72,422.65 at 16:43 EDT on April 9.
