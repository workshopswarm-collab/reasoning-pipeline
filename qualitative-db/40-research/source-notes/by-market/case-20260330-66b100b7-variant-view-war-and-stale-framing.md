## What is uncertain

## Why this source may matter

This note supports the variant claim that the market's discount may be narrative/attention-driven. Traders may be reacting to the salience of war escalation while the actual resolution language is focused on whether a qualifying meeting occurred.

## Possible impact on the question

If the market is mixing up **"Is diplomacy currently progressing?"** with **"Has a qualifying diplomatic meeting occurred?"**, price can remain below fair value even after the underlying condition has likely been met.
