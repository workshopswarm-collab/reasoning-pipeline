# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Labor research should focus on unemployment, participation, wage pressure, structural mismatch, and how labor data changes policy expectations.

## Core conclusions

- Labor is one of the clearest transmission channels between macro conditions and policy.
- Employment weakness and wage dynamics often matter more than a single headline number.
