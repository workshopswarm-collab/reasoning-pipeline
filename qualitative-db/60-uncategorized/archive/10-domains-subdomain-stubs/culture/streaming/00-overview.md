# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Streaming research should focus on subscriber scale, content pipeline, release timing, licensing, ad tiers, and whether platform advantage is durable.

## Core conclusions

- Streaming is now mainstream media infrastructure, not a niche format.
- Platform-level economics matter as much as content-level excitement.
