# Overview summary

## Why this subdomain matters

Robotics research should focus on real-world deployment, safety, autonomy reliability, hardware cost, and whether AI advances are translating into physical-system usefulness.

## Core conclusions

- Robotics is where AI meets operational reality.
- Deployment and reliability matter as much as model capability.
